Ingredients

Rolls

Dough

  • 4 Cups All Purpose Flour
  • 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar
  • 3/4 Tsp Fine Sea Salt
  • 1/3 Cup Unsalted Butter softened
  • 1 Cup Lukewarm (~90¬∞) Whole Milk
  • 2 1/4 Tsp Instant Yeast
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 Egg Yolk

Filling

  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp Cinnamon
  • 1/3 Cup Butter
  • 1 Tbsp Muscovado Sugar (Optional)

Icing

  • 2 Cups Powdered Sugar
  • 2 Tbsp butter melted
  • 2 Tsp Vanilla
  • 4 Tbsp Milk can sub heavy cream or half and half

Steps

  1. Whisk together flour, sugar, and sea salt
  2. Add in butter. Blend in using two forks.
  3. In a separate container, whisk together milk and instant yeast.
  4. Using the dough hook attachment on medium-low speed, add in yeast mixture, eggs, and egg yolk. Mix for 2-3 minutes.
  5. On a lightly floured surface, knead for about a minute, put in greased bowl, cover with damp towel, let rise for 90 minutes.
  6. Once the dough has rise, put on a lightly floured surface and flatten into large rectangle approx. 1/4" thick.
  7. Mix the brown sugar, cinnamon, and muscovado sugar together.
  8. Spread softened butter across the whole rectangle. Then sprinkle cinnamon sugar mix over the whole thing.
  9. Tightly roll dough from the bottom. Slice into 1-2" thick segments.
  10. Grease a pan, place rolls in pan, cover with damp cloth and let rise for 30-45 minutes.
  11. Bake for 15-20 minutes at 375¬∞, let cool in containter for about 15.
  12. Combine powdered sugar, melted butter, and milk in a bowl. Pour over warm rolls and spread.
