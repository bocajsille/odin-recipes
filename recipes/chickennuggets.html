Chicken Nuggets

Description

Crispy. Filling. Delicious.

Ingredients

This is super general, I'm just typing it from memory and don't feel like looking up all the specific spices that goes into the breading
  • 2 Boneless Chicken Breasts
  • Egg Mix
  • Breading Stuff

Steps

  1. Cut chicken into approximately 1 inch big pieces
  2. Dunk chicken in Egg Mix
  3. Roll chicken in Breading Mix
  4. Once all nugs have been breaded, throw them in super hot oil and try not to burn the house down.
