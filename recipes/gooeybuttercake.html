Gooey Butter Cake

Description

This beginner friendly baking recipe is great to showcase your baking skills with a delicious dessert that's sure to be a party favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 Stick of Butter, melted
  • 4 Eggs
  • 8oz. Cream Cheese
  • 1lb. Powdered Sugar
  • 1 Box of Yellow Cake Mix

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°
  2. Grease 9x13 pan
  3. Mix cake mix, 2 eggs, & melted butter in a bowl
  4. Spread mixture evenly on pan
  5. Mix 2 eggs, cream cheese, and powdered sugar in a bowl
  6. Spread over the pre-existing mixture in pan
  7. Bake 30-45 minutes until top of cake is golden
  8. Lightly sprinkle powdered sugar over top of cake
  9. Share with your friends!
